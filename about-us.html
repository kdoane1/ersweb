About Us

ERS Cooling Systems has been providing outstanding service to the mid-atlantic region since 1981. Located just south of Baltimore, ERS is Maryland's largest inventory of heavy duty cooling system products for trucks, equipment and generators. We deal in heavy-duty cooling system parts and service (truck, heavy equipment and generator units). We offer part sales and service for the heavy duty cooling system markets and our warehouse stocks Maryland's largest inventory of heavy duty truck radiators, charge air coolers and ac parts. Our dedication to this field has earned us a reputation of being “radiator specialists”.
